What We Do

Comprehensive State Tax Services

State Income Tax

Multi-state income apportionment, nexus studies, and compliance reviews for corporations and pass-through entities operating across state lines.

Sales & Use Tax

Voluntary disclosure agreements, nexus evaluations, audit defense, and transaction-level review to minimize sales tax exposure and liability.

Nexus & Apportionment

Determine your physical and economic nexus footprint, restructure apportionment factors, and develop state-by-state tax minimization strategies.

Tax Credits & Incentives

Identify and maximize R&D credits, investment incentives, enterprise zone benefits, and other state-level tax credit opportunities for your business.

Audit Defense

Experienced representation before state revenue departments. We manage correspondence, document requests, protests, and appeals to protect your interests.

Voluntary Disclosure

Proactively resolve unfiled state tax obligations through voluntary disclosure programs to limit lookback periods and eliminate penalties.
